This is a package that illustrates what you can achieve with Unity Terrain.  The package includes this demo scene, and supporting assets, such as textures, vegetation models, and height stamps.  These assets are free, and you can use them  in your own projects.

This scene includes a number of bookmarks that appear in the hierarchy under *Bookmarks*.  Select a bookmark to display its associated text (like this). Use the buttons below to advance to the next bookmark, or  position your scene camera to display the point of view associated with each bookmark.  

Click the right arrow to go to the next bookmark in the set. Click the left arrow to go to the previous bookmark.
